About Jena

Hi, I'm Jena Imse — I fix the back end of your business.

Most small business owners are great at what they do. What they're not great at — and shouldn't have to be — is managing messy inventory, building systems from scratch, or training new hires with no documentation to back them up.

That's where I come in. I walk into your business, listen to what's frustrating you, and build practical systems that actually work for your team. Then I hand everything over and you own it — no software subscriptions, no ongoing fees, no dependency on me to keep things running.

"You're paying for expertise and labor, not a platform. Everything we build together is yours to keep."
What I do

Services that actually stick

Hands-on, practical help for the behind-the-scenes stuff that slows your business down.

Inventory cleanup & organization

I audit, sort, label, and systemize your stock so your team can find anything without asking you. No more chaos — just a clean system that works.

Inventory system setup & training

I set up a practical tracking system and train your team to use it. No expensive software, no monthly fees — just something simple that works.

SOP development

I document your key processes in clear step-by-step procedures so your team knows exactly what to do whether you're there or not.

Workflow design

I find where things slow down or get dropped and redesign the flow so your business runs smoother with less confusion.

Standardized hiring processes

I build a repeatable interview and onboarding system so every new hire gets a consistent start and gets up to speed faster.

Operations efficiency audit

I identify the friction points costing you time and money and give you clear recommendations on what to fix first.

Small-task support

Got operational tasks that never get done? I tackle the miscellaneous stuff so you can focus on running your business.
